How to Register

Complete the registration form below for the tournament you wish to participate in. You will receive a confirmation email with payment instructions.

Requirements

Players must have a valid HCA ID or FIDE ID. New players can register for an HCA ID through the form below.

Entry Fees

Entry fees vary by tournament and category. Payment can be made via online transfer or at the venue before the first round.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the registration process?

The registration process involves filling out the online form, receiving a confirmation email, making the payment, and then receiving your tournament ID which you'll need to present at the venue.

How do I make the payment?

After submitting the registration form, you will receive payment instructions via email. You can pay through online transfer (UPI, NEFT, etc.) or at the venue before the first round (subject to availability of spots).

What if I don't have an HCA ID?

New players can register without an HCA ID. Upon registration, you will be assigned a temporary ID for the tournament. To get a permanent HCA ID, you need to submit additional documents which will be specified in your registration confirmation email.

Can I cancel my registration?

Yes, you can cancel your registration up to 48 hours before the tournament starts. Refund policies vary by tournament and will be specified in the tournament details. To cancel, please email us with your registration details.

What should I bring to the tournament?

You should bring your ID proof, registration confirmation, receipt of payment, and your chess equipment (though equipment is usually provided at the venue). It's also advisable to bring water and snacks.
