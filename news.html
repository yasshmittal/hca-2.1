Haryana State Chess Championship 2023 Announced

June 15, 2023 By HCA Admin
Article Image

The Haryana Chess Association is proud to announce the dates for the prestigious Haryana State Chess Championship 2023. The tournament will be held from July 15-20, 2023 in Panchkula.

This championship will serve as a qualification event for the National Chess Championship and will feature the best chess players from across the state competing for the title of Haryana State Champion.

Tournament Details:

  • Dates: July 15-20, 2023
  • Venue: Haryana Chess Academy, Sector 6, Panchkula
  • Format: 9-round Swiss System
  • Time Control: 90 minutes with 30 seconds increment from move 1
  • Prize Fund: ₹100,000

The championship will be conducted in accordance with FIDE Laws of Chess and will be rated by both FIDE and AICF. Players must have a valid AICF registration for the year 2023-24 to participate.

Categories:

The championship will include the following categories:

  • Open Category
  • Women's Category
  • Under-19 Boys and Girls
  • Under-15 Boys and Girls
  • Under-11 Boys and Girls

Registration for the championship will open on June 20, 2023. Players can register online through the HCA website or in person at the HCA office in Panchkula.

For more information, please contact the tournament director at tournaments@hca.org or call the HCA office at +91-1234567890.

We look forward to welcoming all players to this prestigious event and witnessing exciting chess battles to crown the new Haryana State Champions!
