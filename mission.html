Our Mission

Promoting chess excellence and developing chess culture across Haryana

Our Core Mission

"To promote chess as an intellectual pursuit and sport across Haryana, making it accessible to all sections of society while nurturing talent to compete at national and international levels."

The Haryana Chess Association is dedicated to the development and promotion of chess throughout the state of Haryana. We believe that chess is not just a game but a powerful educational tool that enhances critical thinking, concentration, and decision-making skills.

Our mission extends beyond organizing tournaments. We are committed to creating a vibrant chess ecosystem that supports players at all levels, from beginners to grandmasters, and promotes chess as a valuable educational and cultural activity.

Our Mission Pillars

Promote Chess Culture

Establish chess as a mainstream sport and intellectual activity across Haryana by organizing regular tournaments, exhibitions, and public events.

Chess in Education

Integrate chess into school curricula and educational programs to enhance cognitive development, critical thinking, and problem-solving skills among students.

Inclusive Participation

Make chess accessible to all sections of society regardless of age, gender, economic background, or physical abilities through targeted outreach programs.

Talent Development

Identify and nurture chess talent through structured training programs, coaching camps, and competitive opportunities at district, state, and national levels.

Coach Development

Train and certify chess coaches and arbiters to ensure high-quality chess instruction and tournament management across the state.

Partnerships

Collaborate with government bodies, educational institutions, corporate sponsors, and international chess organizations to strengthen chess infrastructure.

Our Strategic Goals

2023-2024

Foundation Building

  • Establish chess clubs in all 22 districts of Haryana
  • Conduct district-level tournaments in each district
  • Train 100+ new chess coaches and arbiters
  • Implement chess programs in 200+ schools
  • Increase registered players by 50%
2025-2026

Growth & Development

  • Host national-level tournaments in Haryana
  • Establish 5 regional high-performance training centers
  • Implement chess in 500+ schools across the state
  • Develop a state chess academy with residential facilities
  • Have 10+ players representing India in international events
2027-2028

Excellence & Recognition

  • Host an international chess tournament in Haryana
  • Produce 5+ International Masters and 1+ Grandmaster
  • Implement chess in all government schools
  • Establish Haryana as a leading chess state in India
  • Create sustainable chess ecosystem with public-private partnerships

Impact Stories

Rural Chess Initiative

Our rural chess initiative has brought chess to over 50 villages in Haryana, providing equipment, training, and regular competitions. This has led to the discovery of several talented young players who are now competing at the state level.

"Chess has transformed our village. Children who once had limited recreational activities now engage in this intellectual sport, showing improved concentration and academic performance."

- Ramesh Singh, Village Sarpanch, Bhiwani District
