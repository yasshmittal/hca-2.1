About Rankings

The Haryana Chess Association rankings are updated monthly based on tournament results and FIDE/AICF rating changes. Players must have participated in at least one rated tournament in the past 12 months to be included in the rankings.

For more detailed player information, click on a player's name to view their complete profile.

Haryana State Chess Championship 2023 Announced

The Haryana Chess Association is proud to announce the dates for the prestigious Haryana State Chess Championship 2023. The tournament will be held from July 15-20, 2023 in Panchkula.

This year's championship will feature separate sections for Open, Women, and Junior categories, with attractive prize funds and FIDE rating opportunities. The tournament will serve as a qualifier for the National Championship.
