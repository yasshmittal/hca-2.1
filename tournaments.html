Host a Tournament

Interested in hosting a chess tournament in your district or club? Haryana Chess Association provides support and guidance for organizing official chess tournaments.

Benefits of Hosting

  • Promote chess in your community
  • Official recognition from HCA
  • Technical support and arbiters
  • Rating processing for FIDE/AICF
  • Publicity through HCA channels

Requirements

  • Suitable venue with adequate space
  • Chess equipment (boards, pieces, clocks)
  • Basic facilities for players
  • Compliance with HCA tournament regulations

Tournament Regulations

  1. The tournament will be conducted as per FIDE Laws of Chess.
  2. Players must arrive at least 15 minutes before the start of each round.
  3. Default time is 30 minutes from the scheduled start of the round.
  4. Electronic devices must be completely switched off and stored as directed by the arbiter.
  5. Tie-breaks will be resolved using: Direct Encounter, Buchholz, Sonneborn-Berger, Progressive Score.
  6. The decision of the Chief Arbiter will be final and binding.
  7. Players must have a valid AICF registration for the current year.
  8. Age proof is mandatory for age-restricted categories.
  9. Entry fees are non-refundable.
  10. The organizers reserve the right to make necessary changes to the schedule if required.
Anti-Cheating Measures
  • Random checks using metal detectors
  • CCTV surveillance in playing hall
  • Anti-cheating software analysis
  • Strict penalties for violations
