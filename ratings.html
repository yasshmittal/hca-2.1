Understanding Chess Ratings

HCA Rating System

The Haryana Chess Association uses a rating system similar to the Elo rating system used by FIDE. Players gain or lose rating points based on their performance against other rated players.

Rating Categories

Players are categorized based on their ratings: Grandmaster (2500+), International Master (2400+), FIDE Master (2300+), Candidate Master (2200+), and Category A-E players.

Rating Calculation

Ratings are calculated after each rated tournament. The change in a player's rating depends on the expected outcome based on rating difference and the actual result.
